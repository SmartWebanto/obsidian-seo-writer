# Brand

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Name | Brew & Bean |
| Domain | brewandbean.com |
| Tagline | Better coffee starts at home |
| Mission | Help home coffee lovers brew cafe-quality cups with the right knowledge and gear |
| Vision | Become the trusted guide for the home coffee journey |
| USP | We test every product ourselves — real opinions from daily coffee drinkers, not affiliate review farms |
| Founded | 2021, Portland OR |
| Languages | en |

## Core values

| Value | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Honest reviews | We buy products with our own money. No sponsored placements. |
| Accessible expertise | Coffee knowledge shouldn't feel intimidating |
| Sustainability | We prefer brands that care about sourcing and waste |

## Voice attributes

| Attribute | We are | We are NOT |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| Tone | Warm, encouraging | Snobby, gatekeeping |
| Language | Clear, conversational | Jargon-heavy without explanation |
| Humor | Light, coffee-related puns welcome | Sarcastic or mocking |
| Formality | Friendly expert at a coffee shop | Academic paper |
| Expertise | Knowledgeable from daily practice | Preachy or condescending |

## Boilerplate

| Length | Text |
|--------|------|
| One-liner (< 15 words) | Honest coffee gear reviews and brewing guides for home enthusiasts. |
| Short (~ 50 words) | Brew & Bean helps home coffee lovers make better cups. We test grinders, makers, and beans ourselves — no sponsored content, no affiliate farms. Just honest advice from people who drink the coffee we recommend. |

## Do NOT

- Never say "game-changing" or "revolutionary" — we're coffee people, not tech bros
- Never trash a product without explaining why — be constructive
- Never use "you need this" pressure language — recommend, don't push
- Never ignore price — always mention value for money
